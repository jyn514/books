# see https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_mathematical_symbols

≡ - congruence
∞ - infinity (or lemniscate)
a, b, c - constants
x, y, z - unknowns
Σ - summation (or sigma)
i - sqrt(-1)
e - base of natural log
ln - natural log (or log base e)
+ - addition or disjoint union, where disjoint union is the union of two sets where a record is kept of from which set a term originated
- - subtraction, negative, or complement, where complement (A, B) is all elements of A not contained in be
